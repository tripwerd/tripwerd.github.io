Trip Werd

Audio Engineer at Chicago Recording Company

Trip Werd is an Audio Engineer and Sound Designer from Chicago, IL, and a graduate of the University of Michigan School of Music in Ann Arbor. Currently an Assistant Engineer for the Audio Post Production department of the world renowned Chicago Recording Company, his work has also been featured at the Palm Springs Film Festival and soon to be on the Big Ten Network.

When not working with waveforms, Trip is an avid music listener of all genres as well as a devout Michigan football fan.
